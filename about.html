LOGIN : As a user, He/She is able to login with valid credentials. As a user, if I entered invalid credentials system should throw error message "Invalid Login Credentials".

LOGOUT : user is able to logout.

REGISTER : New Users are able to register with valid credentials. Email and Date (DOB) Validation applied.

LIST OF MOVIES : list of movies are displayed in the home page

VIEW MOVIE DETAILS : user is able to view movie details.

SEARCH MOVIE BY CITY AND THEATRE : User is able to search movies by city and theatre name. when the city is selected(only after the city selection the theaters seach bar will be enabled) in the search bar the theaters available in that city will be displayed in the next search bar on selection of a theater the movies screening on the same will be displayed

BOOK A TICKET : User should be able to book a ticket. when the city is selected in the search bar the theaters available in that city will be displayed in the next search bar on selection of a theater the movies screening on the same will be displayed , on clicking the image of the movie you'll be redirected to the booking page the seat check boxes will be enabled for seat selection after selection of seats click book button to confirm booking .
